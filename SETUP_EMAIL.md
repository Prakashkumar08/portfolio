# Email Contact System

Your portfolio now uses **Gmail Compose Links** - a simple, reliable way for visitors to contact you!

## How It Works

When visitors submit the contact form or click email links:
1. Gmail opens in a new tab
2. The compose window is pre-filled with their message
3. They click "Send" to complete
4. You receive the email directly in your Gmail inbox: **prakashkumarg0821@gmail.com**

✅ **Mobile Friendly** - Works on all devices
